# BSpec 1.0 Documentation Index

> **Universal Business Specification Standard** - Structured, machine-readable business knowledge graphs

## Overview

BSpec transforms business documentation from scattered artifacts into standardized, interconnected documents that form a complete business knowledge graph. Each document type covers exactly one business concern with explicit relationships to other documents.

**Core Innovation**: Every business is a system of atomic, interconnected documents with rich metadata and relationship mapping.

## Key Concepts

### Document Structure
Each BSpec document contains:
- **YAML frontmatter** with structured metadata
- **Relationship declarations** (depends_on, enables, conflicts_with)
- **Human-readable content** following domain-specific templates
- **Quality standards** (Bronze, Silver, Gold maturity levels)

### Business Knowledge Graph
- **Nodes**: Individual business documents (atomic concerns)
- **Edges**: Explicit relationships between documents
- **Analysis**: Dependency paths, impact assessment, gap detection

### Implementation Approach
1. **Start Small**: Begin with Strategic Foundation documents
2. **Follow Dependencies**: Build prerequisite documents first
3. **Build Domains**: Complete domains based on business priorities
4. **Enhance Quality**: Evolve from Bronze → Silver → Gold maturity
