# Customer & Value Domain

**Domain Overview:** Customer understanding, value analysis, and user experience specifications

## Document Types (12)

### Core Customer Analysis
- **[PER](PER-spec.md)** - Personas: Archetypal customer representations
- **[JTB](JTB-spec.md)** - Jobs-to-be-Done: Functional and emotional jobs
- **[CJM](CJM-spec.md)** - Customer Journey: Experience mapping and touchpoints
- **[USE](USE-spec.md)** - Use Cases: Specific interaction scenarios
- **[STO](STO-spec.md)** - Stories: User story definitions and acceptance criteria

### Pain Points & Value
- **[PAI](PAI-spec.md)** - Pain Points: Customer problems and frustrations
- **[GAI](GAI-spec.md)** - Gains: Customer benefits and value outcomes

### Research & Insights
- **[EMP](EMP-spec.md)** - Empathy Maps: Customer thinking, feeling, seeing, doing
- **[FEE](FEE-spec.md)** - Feedback: Customer input and satisfaction data
- **[INT](INT-spec.md)** - Interviews: Customer research conversations
- **[SUR](SUR-spec.md)** - Surveys: Quantitative customer research
- **[BEH](BEH-spec.md)** - Behavioral Analysis: Customer action patterns

## Domain Purpose

The Customer & Value domain captures customer understanding through personas, journey mapping, jobs-to-be-done analysis, and behavioral research. It connects customer insights to value propositions and guides product development through user-centered design principles.

## Key Relationships

- **Enables**: Product & Service domain specifications
- **Informs**: Business Model value propositions
- **Guides**: Operations & Execution customer processes
- **Connects to**: Strategic Foundation through customer-driven objectives
