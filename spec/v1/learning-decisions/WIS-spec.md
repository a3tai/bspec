# WIS: Wisdom Synthesis Document Type Specification

**Document Type Code:** WIS

**Domain:** Learning & Decisions
**Version:** 1.0.0

**Last Updated:** 2025-09-28

## Purpose and Scope

The Wisdom Synthesis document captures the practical wisdom, judgment principles, and synthesized insights that guide organizational decision-making and action. It establishes wisdom frameworks that combine knowledge, experience, and judgment to create actionable guidance for complex situations, ethical dilemmas, and strategic choices.

## Common Pitfalls

1. **Wisdom hoarding**: Keeping wisdom within small circles rather than sharing broadly
2. **Context blindness**: Applying wisdom without considering situational appropriateness
3. **Dogmatic application**: Treating wisdom as rigid rules rather than flexible principles
4. **Outdated wisdom**: Continuing to apply wisdom that no longer fits current context

## Success Patterns

1. **Living wisdom**: Wisdom that is actively applied and continuously evolved
2. **Contextual application**: Thoughtful adaptation of wisdom to specific situations
3. **Cultural integration**: Wisdom embedded into organizational culture and practices
4. **Generous sharing**: Open sharing of wisdom to benefit the entire organization
