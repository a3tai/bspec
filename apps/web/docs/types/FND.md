# FND: Funding

**Domain**: Financial & Investment

The Funding document defines systematic approaches to raising capital and securing financial resources to support business operations, growth initiatives, and strategic investments. It establishes funding frameworks that optimize capital structure, minimize cost of capital, and align funding strategies with business objectives.

## Metadata Schema

```yaml
---
id: FND-{identifier}
type: FND
status: Draft|Review|Accepted|Deprecated
version: 1.0.0
domain: financial-investment
priority: critical|high|medium|low

depends_on: []
enables: []
---
```

## Quality Levels

| Level | Requirements |
|-------|--------------|
| Bronze | Basic funding strategy with capital requirements analysis, Simple valuation and deal structure framework, Basic due diligence preparation |
| Silver | Comprehensive funding strategy with multiple source analysis, Detailed valuation methodology and negotiation framework, Structured due diligence and investor relations process |
| Gold | Full documentation with validation evidence |

## Relationships

**Depends on**: FIN, VAL, STR

**Enables**: INV, MET, REP
