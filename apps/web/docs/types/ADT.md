# ADT: Adaptation and Agility

**Domain**: Growth & Innovation

The Adaptation and Agility document defines systematic approaches to building organizational capabilities that enable rapid response to changing conditions and emerging opportunities. It establishes agility frameworks that transform organizations into adaptive entities that thrive in uncertainty and complexity.

## Quality Levels

| Level | Requirements |
|-------|--------------|
| Bronze | Basic agility framework with simple sensing and response capabilities, Simple organizational flexibility and change processes, Basic agility culture and mindset development |
| Silver | Comprehensive agility capabilities with systematic sensing and response, Structured organizational design for agility with flexible governance, Active agility culture with resilience and learning capabilities |
| Gold | Full documentation with validation evidence |
