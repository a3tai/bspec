# IFL: Influencer Marketing

**Domain**: Brand & Marketing

The Influencer Marketing document defines strategies for partnering with influencers to amplify brand messaging, build credibility, and reach target audiences through authentic endorsements. It establishes influencer frameworks that create genuine partnerships, drive engagement, and generate measurable business value through strategic influencer collaboration.

## Quality Levels

| Level | Requirements |
|-------|--------------|
| Bronze | Basic influencer marketing strategy with selection criteria and partnership models, Simple content collaboration framework and performance tracking, Basic legal compliance and relationship management processes |
| Silver | Comprehensive influencer marketing strategy with detailed discovery and selection, Structured content collaboration with performance optimization, Active program management with analytics and relationship building |
| Gold | Full documentation with validation evidence |
