# BPO: Brand Positioning

**Domain**: Brand & Marketing

The Brand Positioning document defines how the brand occupies a distinctive position in customers' minds relative to competitors. It establishes positioning frameworks that create clear differentiation, build competitive advantage, and guide all marketing communications to ensure consistent market perception and customer preference.

## Quality Levels

| Level | Requirements |
|-------|--------------|
| Bronze | Basic brand positioning with clear statement and competitive frame, Simple differentiation strategy and basic validation, Basic implementation guidelines and measurement approach |
| Silver | Comprehensive brand positioning with detailed competitive analysis, Structured perception management with measurement framework, Active positioning implementation with monitoring and optimization |
| Gold | Full documentation with validation evidence |
