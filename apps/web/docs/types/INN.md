# INN: Innovation Strategy

**Domain**: Growth & Innovation

The Innovation Strategy document defines systematic approaches to identifying, developing, and scaling innovation opportunities that create competitive advantage and drive sustainable growth. It establishes innovation frameworks that transform innovation from random creativity into systematic business capability.

```yaml
---

---
```

## Quality Levels

| Level | Requirements |
|-------|--------------|
| Bronze | Basic innovation strategy with portfolio framework, Simple innovation process and project management, Basic innovation culture initiatives and recognition |
| Silver | Comprehensive innovation strategy with balanced portfolio management, Structured innovation process with validation and scaling methodologies, Active innovation culture with external partnerships and networks |
| Gold | Full documentation with validation evidence |

## Relationships

**Depends on**: STR, RND, EXP

**Enables**: PRD, SVC, FUT
