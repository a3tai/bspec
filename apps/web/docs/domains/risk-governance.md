# Risk & Governance

The Risk & Governance domain covers organizational risk management and governance structures. These documents define risk assessments, compliance requirements, and control frameworks.

## Document Types (8)

| Code | Name | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| [COM](/docs/types/COM) | Compliance | Regulatory compliance documentation |
| [CTL](/docs/types/CTL) | Controls | Internal controls and safeguards |
| [ETH](/docs/types/ETH) | Ethics | Ethical guidelines and standards |
| [GOV](/docs/types/GOV) | Governance | Governance structures and policies |
| [INC](/docs/types/INC) | Incidents | Incident response and management |
| [INS](/docs/types/INS) | Insurance | Insurance coverage and risk transfer |
| [LEG](/docs/types/LEG) | Legal | Legal requirements and documentation |
| [RSK](/docs/types/RSK) | Risks | Risk identification and assessment |

## Key Relationships

Risk & Governance documents typically:
- **Depend on**: Operations, Technology, and Financial data
- **Enable**: Compliance, Security, and Stakeholder confidence
