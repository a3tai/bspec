# Growth & Innovation

The Growth & Innovation domain covers how your organization evolves and grows. These documents define innovation strategies, experimentation frameworks, and future planning.

## Document Types (7)

| Code | Name | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| [ADT](/docs/types/ADT) | Adaptation and Agility | Organizational adaptability and change management |
| [EXP](/docs/types/EXP) | Experimentation | Testing and experimentation frameworks |
| [FUT](/docs/types/FUT) | Future Planning | Long-term vision and scenario planning |
| [IGN](/docs/types/IGN) | Insight Generation | Research and insight development |
| [INN](/docs/types/INN) | Innovation Strategy | Innovation approach and pipeline |
| [LEA](/docs/types/LEA) | Learning Organization | Organizational learning practices |
| [RND](/docs/types/RND) | Research and Development | R&D programs and investments |

## Key Relationships

Growth & Innovation documents typically:
- **Depend on**: Strategy, Market insights, and Customer feedback
- **Enable**: Product evolution and Competitive advantage

## Getting Started

For Silver conformance, add:
1. **INN** - Define your innovation strategy
2. **EXP** - Establish experimentation practices
